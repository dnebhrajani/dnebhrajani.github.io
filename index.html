About

My hometown, California:

I’m a self-trained artist with a passion for sketching and portraiture in charcoal and graphite. From finger-drawings on a freshly-painted wall, to lettering quotes in a skating rink and painting my shoes, art has been consistent with me for as long as I can remember. While I’ve tried several different styles and mediums, including quilling paper strips, origami, acrylic, watercolour, ink, and a few others, there’s something about being able to generate art with nothing more than a pencil that has an irresistible hold on me.

Education

I studied in California until the first grade. After moving to India, I continued my education at The Orchid School, where I completed grades 1 through 12. I’ve always had a strong passion for science and mathematics, and my academic pursuits consistently revolved around these subjects. I’m currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science at IIIT-Hyderabad.

Achievements

My achievements span academics, athletics, and art. I’ve received awards in science fairs, math quizzes, and art competitions during my school years. I was also a qualifier for the Indian National Olympiad in Informatics (INOI) through the Zonal Informatics Olympiad (ZIO). In addition, I’ve earned accolades in sports at both the district and university levels. A detailed list of these accomplishments can be found in my CV below.

Technical Skills

My technical skills, as outlined in my CV, include programming in languages such as C, C++, and Python. I also have experience with web development using HTML, CSS, Markdown, and Pandoc, as well as database management in both MySQL and NoSQL systems. These skills reflect my current proficiency as a first-year Computer Science student, having acquired them through recent academic and project-based learning.
